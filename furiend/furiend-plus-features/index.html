What's included with Furiend+ ?

Keep track of the activites you have to do or already did with your pets.

Keep track of the trainings you have to do or already did with your pets.

Have interactable reminders for feeding and other activities.

No worries if you have more then one pet at home. You can track all of their activites with Furiend+

Shareable data here in Furiend+ allows you to share tracking data with anyone you would want to share with.

Easy data syncing allows you to be up to date on your tracking data on all your devices.
