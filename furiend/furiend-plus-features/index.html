Furiend+ Benefits

One subscription to make pet management simple and easy.

What's included with Furiend+ ?

    Track Activities

Track all your pet’s activities from potty to grooming.

    Custom Activities

Don’t see an activity you would like to track? Set up your own customise activity and start tracking activities that include distance, duration, volume or all of them!

    Monitor training progress

Track and monitor the progress of your pet’s training. Personalised ratings shows how well the pet is following the commands.

    Reminders

Set up schedules for repeating activities like yearly vaccinations and daily feeding. You can even set-up custom reminders for all your other pet’s needs.

    Manage Multiple Pets

Do everything and more with all your other pets. Create and manage multiple pet details all in one single app!

    Share pet data with other paw-parents

Share your pet’s information with caretakers or other paw parents.

    Sync across devices

All your data is safe in the cloud and can be synced across multiple devices.

    Temperature tracking

Track your pet’s temperature and capture other important details along with it.

    Ad-free experience

Support the app you love and avoid the ads you hate.
