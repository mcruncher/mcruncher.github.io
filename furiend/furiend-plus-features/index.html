Furiend+ Features

Data Sharing

It takes a village to raise a pet! Furiend makes it easy for family members to collaboratively manage their pet's well-being and ensure that everyone has access to accurate and up-to-date information. Subscription plans can be shared with your family.

Activity Tracker

With our Activity tracker, you can track your pet's daily activities such as bath, poo, and pee. You can set up reminders and log them as they are done so you are on track with your pet's needs in a timely manner.

Training Tracker

Training plays an essential role in maintaining your pet’s health. Now, you can track your pet’s training progress like never before. Whether you’re teaching basic obedience commands or working on advanced tricks, Furiend is your reliable companion for tracking and enhancing your pet’s training experience.

Temperature

Furiend helps you keep a record of your pet’s temperature as well as any other observed symptoms. Whether your pet is recovering from an illness, experiencing a change in health, or simply going for a regular health check-up, Furiend keeps you informed of your pet’s health.

Medication History

With its intuitive interface, you can easily input and update the details of medications, including dosage, frequency, and duration. Furiend provides timely reminders for administering medications, ensuring that you never miss a dose. It also keeps a comprehensive record of your pet’s medicine history, allowing you to track the effectiveness of treatments
